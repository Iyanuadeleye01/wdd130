Our Rafting Trips

Ready for an adventure? Contact Us today to book your trip!

Rapid Rush Expedition

Experience the thrill of conquering roaring Class IV rapids on this exhilarating 2-day expedition deep through the heart of the canyon. Paddle alongside our expert guides as you navigate powerful currents, challenging drops, and breathtaking white water sections. By day, feel the rush of adrenaline as you tackle some of the river’s most exciting stretches. By night, relax at a scenic riverside campsite under the stars, enjoying delicious meals and stories by the campfire. This trip is perfect for adventure seekers ready to push their limits and make unforgettable memories on the water

Family Float Adventure

Perfect for beginners and families, this calm and relaxing float trip lets you soak in stunning scenery while drifting along gentle rapids that are safe and fun for all ages. Glide past lush riverbanks, watch for local wildlife, and enjoy the peaceful sounds of nature as you float downstream. Our friendly guides ensure a safe, enjoyable ride, sharing fun facts about the river and its surroundings along the way. With plenty of opportunities to splash around and take photos, this adventure promises smiles, laughter, and lasting memories for both kids and adults alike

Extreme White Water Challenge

For adrenaline junkies only! Get ready to conquer the river’s fiercest and most demanding rapids on this intense, full-day white water challenge. Brace yourself for non-stop action as you tackle churning Class IV and V rapids, steep drops, and powerful waves that will keep your heart racing from start to finish. Under the guidance of our highly trained expert team, you will learn advanced paddling techniques and safety tips to master each rapid with confidence.
